Major Projects from AIE

VRIG (Alpha Phase)

VRIG is a VR interaction system built on the Lumberyard engine as a Gem to support the development of future VR games. The gem is designed to be as simple as possible to add to projects and rapidly test with the ability to program in both Lua or Script Canvas to handle common VR events such as hovering, selecting, and activating game objects for both Oculus and Open VR platforms.

Watch

Meltdown VR

Meltdown VR is a game where players must stop a nuclear power plant from exploding by performing a series of seemingly innocuous tasks. Worked on major systems within the Unity Engine, focusing on VR interactions and game controllers using Valve's SteamVR plugin for cross-VR-platform functionality.
